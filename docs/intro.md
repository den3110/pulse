# Introduction to Pulse

Welcome to Pulse, the modern and lightweight remote deployment platform for managing VPS instances seamlessly.
Designed as a high-performance alternative to traditional panels, Pulse makes server management a breeze for solo developers and teams alike.

## Why Pulse?

Unlike heavy control panels that consume gigabytes of RAM in the background, Pulse focuses on a localized CLI and a central management hub.
This ensures your target VPS remains strictly dedicated to your applications, not the panel itself.

- **Zero Overhead:** Agent-less design means no daemon stealing your production server's resources.
- **Git-Ops Native:** Connect your repositories and trigger automated deployments effortlessly.
- **Modern Architecture:** Built with Node.js, React, and MUI; supporting real-time WebSocket syncing.
